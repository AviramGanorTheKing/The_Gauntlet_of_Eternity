# THE HOOK — Unified Synthesis from 8 Research Teams

## Research Date: March 2, 2026

---

## The One-Sentence Pitch

**"A dungeon crawler where your party members have opinions about you — and the dungeon is watching."**

All 8 research teams independently converged: the combination of **AI companions with personality/loyalty/betrayal** + **an announcer who is a character, not a narrator** + **moral choices with real consequences** does not exist anywhere in gaming.

---

## The Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: "The Party That Judges You"
- Companion Loyalty score (0-100) shifts based on actions
- Selfish choices: companions hoard, steal, sabotage, eventually betray
- Selfless choices: companions become synergistic heroes
- ~20% of runs: one companion secretly corrupted (hidden traitor with behavioral sabotage)
- Announcer narrates companion dynamics: "The Wizard seems... displeased with you."

### Pillar 2: "The Dungeon Is Alive"
- Biomes 1-3: Announcer reliable
- Biome 4: Announcer occasionally lies
- Biome 5: Announcer IS the Void Architect. Gaslights player. False HUD values. Scrambled minimap.
- CRT shader = the corruption. Increases as you descend. Death = CRT breaks uniquely per damage type.

### Pillar 3: "Every Death Is A Story"
- Near-miss death screen: "Bone Sovereign: 8% HP remaining"
- Death Card: CRT-styled shareable run summary (one-click copy)
- Post-death exclusive lore
- Sub-10-second death-to-gameplay
- The Dungeon Remembers: corpse appears next run (micro moral choice)

---

## The Addiction Engine

- **Synergy "Aha" Moments:** 10+ cross-system interactions to discover. "SYNERGY!" popup. Build names.
- **Power Fantasy Curve:** Exponential. Damage numbers scale visually. "Power Room" per biome.
- **Zeigarnik Unfinished Business:** Always 3+ open loops at quit time.
- **Loot Reveal Delay:** 0.5s rarity beam before item resolves.
- **Near-Miss Architecture:** Show exactly how close on death. 5-10% "Heartbreak" reveals.

---

## The Spawner Loop (Lost Gauntlet Magic)

- Room completion gated by spawner destruction, not kill count
- Spawners accelerate output over time
- Different types require different approaches
- Knockback enemies INTO spawners for bonus damage
- Announcer calls out locations: "Generators detected!"

## The Bottom Line

> A browser dungeon crawler where you lead a party of AI companions who develop opinions about your choices, narrated by an announcer who slowly reveals itself as your enemy, in a dungeon that remembers how you died — all wrapped in a CRT aesthetic that IS the corruption, not just decoration.

**Hades' narrative depth + Gauntlet's party chaos + Undertale's moral weight + Inscryption's fourth-wall breaks. In a browser. For free.**
