# Roguelike Market Research & Positioning (Round 1)

## Research Date: March 2, 2026

---

## What Makes Each Hit Memorable (One Hook Per Game)

- **Balatro:** "The numbers go insane" — transparent math makes explosions comprehensible and shareable
- **Hades:** "Death IS the story" — every failure advances narrative
- **Slay the Spire 2:** "Co-op deckbuilding" — 4-player with multiplayer-specific cards
- **Vampire Survivors:** "You just move" — radical input removal + escalation power fantasy
- **Caves of Qud:** "Emergent stories" — deeply simulated world writes itself

**Pattern:** The hook is always ONE axis of escalation that produces surprising, shareable moments.

---

## Market Gaps

1. **"Gauntlet Fantasy" abandoned** — No modern party-based spawner-destruction roguelike since 2014
2. **AI companions that feel real** — Most are passive buffs or turrets
3. **Moral choices in roguelikes** — Almost nonexistent
4. **CRT as gameplay, not filter** — Underexploited
5. **Web-first roguelikes** — $10.5B market, almost all hits are desktop/console

---

## Five Unique Mechanic Ideas

### A. "The Companion Dilemma" (HIGH IMPACT, LOW EFFORT)
Companions develop opinions about moral choices. Selfish = they hoard, steal, sabotage. Selfless = synergistic heroes.
- `companionLoyalty` score (0-100). Moral choices shift it. Gates AI behaviors.
- Low-loyalty: refuse revival, leave party.

### B. "Corruption Temptation" (HIGH IMPACT, MEDIUM EFFORT)
Corrupted gear drops: dramatically overpowered but stacking CRT glitch penalties. At max corruption (3 items): become a mini-boss for future runs. Hidden 6th ending.

### C. "The Dungeon Remembers" (MEDIUM IMPACT, LOW EFFORT)
Previous death room becomes mini-event next run. Loot corpse (ghost fights), free soul (stat buff), or ignore (reveals map). Moral micro-choice on every death.

### D. "Daily Gauntlet" (MEDIUM IMPACT, LOW EFFORT)
Daily seeded run, global leaderboard, same dungeon for everyone.

### E. "Announcer Escalation" (LOW IMPACT, VERY LOW EFFORT)
Hidden "impressed" counter. Killstreaks = progressively excited lines. Poor play = passive-aggressive ("perhaps try the tutorial?").

---

## Retention Keys

- Core loop under 20 minutes per biome arc
- Variable reinforcement (jackpot room ~1/15)
- Instant restart (death → gameplay under 10 seconds)
- Meta-progression doesn't invalidate skill
- "I Almost Had It" moment (visible boss HP, "almost dead" state at Phase 3)

---

## Recommendation

**The companions are the hook.** No roguelike has AI companions with emergent personality, loyalty, and betrayal. Combined with moral choices and announcer commentary, this creates "let me tell you what happened" moments no other game produces.
